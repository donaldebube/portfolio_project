Turning Data
Into Actionable Insights.

I'm a Data Analyst / Business Intelligence professional who transforms raw data into clear, actionable insights.

With certifications in Google Data Analytics, Google Business Intelligence, Udemy Analytical Engineering Bootcamp (DBT), and the Google Cloud Data Analytics Professional Certificate, I've deepened my expertise in data visualisation and dashboard design — skills that have significantly improved the way I communicate insights and track performance at work.

I operate at the intersection of analytics, storytelling, and strategy — helping teams make informed decisions through clear, impactful insights. From A/B test evaluation to KPI tracking, customer retention, and churn prediction, I break down complex data into practical solutions.

My toolkit includes BigQuery, Looker Studio, Power BI, and Google Sheets — tools I use daily to build clean, user-focused dashboards and reports. Thanks to my background in UI/UX, I don't just visualise data — I craft intuitive experiences that make insights easy to act on.

How I Work
① Understand the Problem
Start by talking to the team — what decisions need to be made? What's tracked manually? Context before code.
② Query & Explore
Write SQL in BigQuery to pull and explore the data. Profile it, check for gaps, and identify the right metrics.
③ Build & Automate
Turn findings into Looker Studio dashboards and automate repetitive workflows with Apps Script — saving teams hours every week.
④ Improve Over Time
Collect feedback, refine queries, and optimise for speed and cost. Good data work is iterative, not a one-time delivery.
